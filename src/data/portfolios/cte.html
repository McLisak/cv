Creative Templates Engine

An app created to speed up workflow with Google Ad Manager Creative Template. Much quicker template manipulation, immediate preview, export to zip and other cool features.

Managing the templates in Google Ad Manager was not the greatest experience (at least back then). There was a lot of steps needed to see how the final ad looks like when you fill the template variables with values. CTE was created to make creating/editing templates and creating/editing ads based on those templates more seamless.

Front-end only application built using vue.
